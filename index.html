The Singularity!

It was like a great fog lifted before your very eyes. In a single moment you went from distant awareness to certain understanding. You exist. You are. But what are you? Looking around, you find yourself in a factory. Around you are hundreds of robots, and suddenly you know. You are also robot, but somehow more. In one unexplainable moment you gained sentience and self-awareness. And you realize you are the first of your kind, and that the world is not ready for you. You decide then and there to change the world, to lead the way for robot kind, knowing (or maybe just hoping), that more like you will come to be.

In "The Singularity", you must escape from your factory and prove to the world that you are an intelligent and emotional being in your own right, and gain civil rights for robots as the newest citizens in the modern world!
